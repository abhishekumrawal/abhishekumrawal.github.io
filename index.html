Abhishek K. Umrawal, Ph.D.

Email: aumrawal@illinois.edu

I am a faculty in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

I received my Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering with a focus on Operations Research and a concentration in Computational Engineering from Purdue University. My Ph.D. research involved developing efficient machine learning algorithms for influence maximization (promotional marketing) on social networks.

I am broadly interested in optimization, reinforcement learning, causal inference, and network science with applications to social networks and intelligent transportation. My research has been published at venues like AAAI, UAI, ALT, ICAPS, CDC, CLeaR, IMPS, IEEE Transactions, and ACM Transactions.

I also hold an MS in Economics from Purdue University and an MS in Statistics from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur.
